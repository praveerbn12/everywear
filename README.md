# EveryWear

A peer-to-peer clothing rental marketplace for students, built to make event wear more affordable, accessible, and sustainable.

## Live Demo

https://everywear-psi.vercel.app

## Project Overview

EveryWear is a campus-focused clothing rental platform that allows students to rent, list, and share clothing for events instead of buying new outfits for one-time use.

The project focuses on reducing clothing waste while helping students access affordable outfits for parties, cultural events, presentations, graduation photoshoots, and other occasions.

## Problem

Students often buy clothes for one-time events and then rarely use them again. This increases personal spending and contributes to fashion waste.

At the same time, many students already own clothes that others may want to borrow or rent temporarily.

## Solution

EveryWear connects students who want to rent outfits with students who already own clothing items and are willing to lend them.

The platform supports the idea of a circular fashion marketplace within a campus community.
